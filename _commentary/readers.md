My descriptions are intended to be as accessible as possible to readers without any training in computer science or classical AI.

Since Toroidal is a Rubik's Cube variant, there's little novelty in the application of well-known AI techniques to this game, so there's equally little reason to dress these descriptions up in academic garb.

I think it's possible to gain a little insight into some of the fundamentals of computer science and classical AI, and then go on to more formal venues to learn more about them.

But I am pretty sure my descriptions will have a very small readership.  That's okay by me.
