The earliest articles on this website are dated in summer and fall of 2023.  The next articles are dated early in 2025.  

In late October of 2023, I moved to a new city. For much of 2024, I was settling in, and developing new routines.  I worked only sporadically on this project.  And a lot of what I was doing was software engineering, and not writing technical prose.

During this hiatus, the free web-hosting service I had been using shut down, and so in the late summer of 2024 I had to rebuild my website, using a new host.  Of course, I chose one that required me to learn a whole bunch of new stuff.  Good mental exercise, but not the quickest route.

In early 2025, a colleague of mine expressed interest in this project, so I felt I would tidy it up a bit, and then point him to it.  Well, I am still tidying!
